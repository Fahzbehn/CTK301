Welcome to Coyote Crossing!

Welcome to Coyote Crossing, New Mexico's newest campgrounds. We're situated smack dab in the middle of nowhere, an hour's drive from Sante Fe. We have forty RV/tent sites available, vault restrooms, potable water on the premises, and twenty acres of hiking trails. We're pet friendly with a dog park on site. Come visit the beautiful scenic views with abundant wildlife viewing areas, gorgeous sunsets and dark star filled skies. We can't wait to have you join us out here at Coyote Crossing!
