In Rhythm City Racers, you play a Rhythm Rebel, racing against the forces of banality trying to wipe out all originality from Rhythm City. Each stay will have you racing against procedurally generated stages that are based off the music you listen to. Be warned, enemies will also block your path, so you will need to attack them while constantly moving forward to get through each stage of this rhythm runner game.

Available for alpha release: March 2022!

In Dream Supreme, you play a dreamer exploring a constantly expanding dreamscape. Along the way, you will face nightmare after nightmare until the hunter is revealed. Then it's a mad dash back to the safety of your bedroom before the hunter captures you and your friends.

Available for Purchase: January 2022!
