About Us

Everwynd Publishing is a multiple format media company focused on creative technologies. From our ongoing webcomics to web development and game design, we pride ourselves on a broad range of medias. Originally founded by Rick Starkweather, author of "The Howling Heart", we strive to entertain and educate.

From game and sound design to narrative fiction, we here at Everwynd Publishing are here to help you with your media needs. We are currently prototyping two games for hopeful release in early 2022: "Rhythm City Runner" and "Dream Supreme". For information on both products, please check our games tab or click here.
